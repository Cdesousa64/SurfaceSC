üì• Inventory Services Newsflash (May 2025)
Forecasting VS Bill and Hold

Forecasting: This is a value-add service offered to Softchoice customers who have identified a specific business requirement to have Softchoice reserve inventory on behalf of their customer based on the customer commitment of purchase the inventory within 60 days
  • Inventory reserved in advance based on customer purchase commitment
  • Free warehousing for up to 60 days
  • Invoiced at time of shipment to customer
Bill and Hold: This is a value-add service offered to Softchoice customers who have identified a specific business requirement for warehousing and provides a Purchase order a bulk for their inventory. This service is used when the customer requires their products/goods stored and then shipped to their desired location(s).
  • Products stored upon bulk purchase order
  • Warehousing fees apply from Day 1 (monthly charges based on quantity/duration)
  • Invoiced when inventory is physically available; ownership begins at that point
  • Ideal for staged rollouts or multi-location deliveries
